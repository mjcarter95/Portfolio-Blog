Effortlessly elevate your salads with to the next level this bold and tangy dressing.

Ingredients

* dijon mustard
* lemon juice
* honey
* apple cider vinegar
* shallots
* olive oil
* salt
* black pepper

Preparation\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Add ingredients to a container. Place the lid on the container and shake to emulsify. Enjoy!
