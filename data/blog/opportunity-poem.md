These words are in memory of opportunities lost,\
For opportunities seen but not seized.\
The unanswered question: what could have been?

These words are in memory of opportunities lost,\
A phone call, a kiss.\
Lacking courage, the moment now missed.

These words are to celebrate opportunities to come,\
For opportunities unseen, songs yet to be sung.\
Painting on life's canvas, where hopes and dreams are begun.

MJC
