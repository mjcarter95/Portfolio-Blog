You ain't got a leg to stand on,
Croaked the old man to the boy.
You've turned into a cynic,
When you were once so filled with joy.
What stole away your laughter, lad?
Your spirit danced with glee.
Now you've lost your spark, your light,
You’re shrouded in black,
Sat here,
With me.
